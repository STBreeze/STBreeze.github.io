Project Overview

Traditional solar panels remain fixed, limiting their efficiency as the sun moves, while dust accumulation and environmental wear further degrade performance and increase maintenance needs. This project introduces an automated solar tracker with a collapsible housing unit that maximizes energy capture by continuously aligning with the sun, opens during the day for power generation, and closes at night to protect the panels from dust and wear. Utilizing a hinged, book-like motion, the system ensures smooth, efficient operation, reducing maintenance costs and enhancing long-term durability, making solar power more efficient, reliable, and sustainable.

One of the biggest challenges in solar energy systems is efficiency loss due to suboptimal sun exposure and environmental degradation. Two key issues impact the performance of harnessing solar power:

üî¥ Fixed-Angle Limitation: Most solar panels are installed at a fixed tilt, preventing them from capturing optimal sunlight throughout the day.

üî¥ Environmental Wear & Dust Accumulation: Solar panels are exposed to dust and rough weather, leading to decreased efficiency and increased maintenance costs.

üåü The Solution? A smart solar tracker with a collapsible housing that ensures:

  • Panels always face the sun for maximum energy absorption.
  • Protection at night, reducing wear & tear and maintenance needs.

üìä How It Works: Methodology & Design

1Ô∏è‚É£ Sun Tracking Mechanism
  • The SPA detects the sun's position using the GPS/Time module.
  • The difference between the sun & solar panel position is calculated.
  • Motors align the panels to reduce this difference, tracking the sun's movement.
  • Real-time adjustments optimize energy capture throughout the day.
2Ô∏è‚É£ Collapsible Housing System
  • The housing remains open during daylight hours.
  • At sunset, the rack pinion mechanism engages a hinge system, closing the housing like a book.
  • This prevents dust accumulation, weather damage, and unnecessary night time exposure.
3Ô∏è‚É£ Motion Control & Automation
  • Microcontrollers & GPS/Time module & servo motors automate the tracking & folding system.
  • Pre-programmed logic determines optimal tracking angles & closure timing.
  • Energy-efficient actuation ensures minimal power consumption.
